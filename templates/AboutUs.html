Company Story

In the year 2007, in the heart of Silicon Valley, MKW was born. Our founders, Mickey and Chuan, two engineers from prestigious tech universities, shared a vision of creating technology solutions that could change the world by assists companies of all sizes and across all sectors in modernising their HR functions to increase efficiency.

Initially, the firm relied on a Windows-based HR System. As time passed, the company's reputation blossomed, and its client base expanded. Recognizing a shifting economic landscape and evolving industry trends, we seized the opportunity to transition from a Windows-based system to a Cloud-based Human Resource Management Software (HRMS). This revolutionary shift was successfully carried out in 2014, beginning with our operations in India. The introduction of our cloud-based HRMS not only marked a significant milestone in our journey but also catalyzed our expansion into other Asian markets, including China, Thailand, and Korea. Fast forward to 2023, our company boasts a dedicated workforce of over 300 individuals. We are proud to serve more than 30,000 clients and handle the payroll administration for over half a million employees. Our continued growth is a testament to our commitment to innovation and service excellence in the field of HR technology.
